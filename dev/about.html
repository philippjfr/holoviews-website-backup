About Us¶

HoloViews is part of the IOAM, the Github organization for the Computational Systems Neuroscience Group at the University of Edinburgh. We are members of the Edinburgh Doctoral Training Center in Neuroinformatics and the Edinburgh Institute for Adaptive and Neural Computation.

When we are not working on HoloViews, we are computational neuroscientists working on large-scale models of the mammalian visual system. We are also developers of the Topographica neural simulator which we mostly use to build computational models of the primary visual cortex (V1).

Jean-Luc Stevens : PhD candidate investigating the temporal dynamics of V1 activity response.

Philipp Rudiger : PhD candidate investigating anatomically realistic models of V1 development and how these relate to natural image statistics.

James A. Bednar: Associate Professor and Director of the DTC, researching biologically realistic computational models of human and animal visual systems.

HoloViews also includes code from Christopher Ball and Jefferson Provost.
